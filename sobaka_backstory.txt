Umniy Sobaka — Ghost Alchemist Backstory

• Raised inside Mother's sentient chicken-legged hut; learned alchemy amid strange magic.
• During wife's childbirth the hut was besieged; Sobaka fought to defend family.
• He died in the assault, awakening as a ghost sustained by Mother's magic.
• Now incorporeal: can interact with objects only through a gifted pouch holding his mask.
• Goal: find and protect his wife, daughter, and the wandering hut.
