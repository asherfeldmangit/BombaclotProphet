Orpheus & Stella Belcourt — Twin Backstory

• Fetchling twins from a brothel in Mechitar.
    – Orpheus: 32, flirtatious rogue-duelist, worships Calistria.
    – Stella: 32, reserved animist, worships Pharasma.
• Grew up amid undead labor and excess; disillusioned with Geb's cruelty.
• Stella touched the Cinerarium pyramid, gained spirit-sight and occult powers.
• Orpheus mastered high-society charm and dueling to protect them.
• Mother was daylight-attacked by an unknown vampire; guards ignored the case.
• Twins swore a blood pact to champion the common folk and seek change.
• Current Goal: become Blood Lords to secure rights for the oppressed living.
